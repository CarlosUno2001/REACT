What is the story about?

The story begins at La Naval Academy, where Principal Lopez introduces the Eco Heroes Challenge. Students are tasked with designing a solution to help the environment by creating a diorama or model. Mia/Leo, the protagonist, initially feels unsure or indifferent about the project. However, Principal Lopez encourages them to see the opportunity as a chance to make a real impact. With the support of their friend Kara, the protagonist decides to participate and starts brainstorming ideas for a project.

The journey involves finding useful materials for the protagonist's diorama, exploring their city with Kara, and meeting and interacting with other members of their community as they go along.

With winning the Eco Heroes Challenge in mind, the protagonist finishes their adventure around the community with a diorama of a machine that could help the environment, gaining new friends and learning lessons along their way.
